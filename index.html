About Me

I am a graduated Physics student from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong and currently working as research assistant that focussing on implementing deep learning models on weather forecasting task under the supervision of Prof. Yeung Dit-Yan in HKUST. I received Bachelor of Science with major in Phyiscs from HKUST in 2022.

My current research interest are model robustness and model watermarking. I did the work on developing a new loss function to improve the the performance of video prediction models.
